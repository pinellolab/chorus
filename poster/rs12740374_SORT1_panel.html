What the predictions say

  1. The minor (T) allele opens chromatin at the enhancer (DNase, both oracles: ChromBPNet +1.24, AlphaGenome +1.34).
  2. It gains a CEBPA binding site (ChromBPNet log₂FC +1.99, AlphaGenome +2.7; orthogonal models agree).
  3. CEBPA recruits the active enhancer mark H3K27ac (+1.27).
  4. eRNA at the enhancer jumps (CAGE +1.47 right at the variant site).
  5. Effect on SORT1’s own TSS is directionally UP but small (~1.05 to 1.15×); closer eQTL genes CELSR2 and PSRC1 are predicted to rise more.

Ground truth (Musunuru et al., Nature 2010)

  • Minor T allele creates a C/EBP site in a hepatocyte enhancer.
  • SORT1 ↑ in liver, leading to reduced VLDL secretion.
  • Plasma LDL-C ↓, and CAD risk ↓.
  • Direction and identity of the bound TF were validated experimentally.
